Our Story

New Designing studio

New Designing Studio, located in the vibrant Bazaar Road of Madhavaperumalpuram, Mylapore, is your trusted destination for custom clothing and designer stitching. With a passion for fashion and a commitment to affordability, we bring your dream outfits to life with precision and creativity. Whether it's a traditional ensemble or a contemporary design, our skilled team works closely with you to deliver garments that fit perfectly and reflect your personal style.

Serving the Chennai community with dedication, New Designing Studio has built a reputation for great designs and customized dresses at prices that don't break the bank. We are open seven days a week from 9:00 AM to 10:00 PM, making it convenient for you to visit, consult, and get your outfits crafted with care. Our studio is a go-to tailoring destination in Mylapore for those who value quality workmanship and unique designs.
